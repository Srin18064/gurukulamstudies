Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Gurukulam Studies offer?
Gurukulam Studies provides comprehensive educational consultancy services, including university selection, application assistance, visa guidance, and pre-departure support for students aiming to study in Vietnam.
Which universities in Vietnam do you work with?
We partner with top universities in Vietnam, including Vietnam National University, Hanoi University of Science and Technology, and RMIT University Vietnam, among others.
How do I apply for a university in Vietnam?
Our team will guide you through the entire application process, from selecting the right program to submitting your application and required documents.
Do I need a visa to study in Vietnam?
Yes, international students require a student visa to study in Vietnam. We provide assistance with the visa application process to ensure a smooth experience.
What are the language requirements for studying in Vietnam?
Most programs are offered in English, so proficiency in English is required. Some universities may ask for IELTS or TOEFL scores. For Vietnamese-taught programs, you may need to demonstrate Vietnamese language proficiency.
Can I work while studying in Vietnam?
Yes, international students are allowed to work part-time while studying in Vietnam, subject to certain restrictions. We can provide more details based on your specific situation.
What is the cost of studying in Vietnam?
The cost varies depending on the university and program. On average, tuition fees range from $2,000 to $10,000 per year. We can help you find a program that fits your budget.
How can I contact Gurukulam Studies for more information?
You can reach us via email at info@gurukulamstudies.com or call us at +84 123 456 789. Our office is located in Hanoi, Vietnam, and we also offer online consultations.
